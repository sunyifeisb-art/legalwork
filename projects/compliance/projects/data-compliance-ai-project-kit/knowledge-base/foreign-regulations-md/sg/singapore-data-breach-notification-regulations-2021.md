# Personal Data Protection (Notification of Data Breaches) Regulations 2021

Jurisdiction: Singapore
Region: SG
Citation: Personal Data Protection (Notification of Data Breaches) Regulations 2021
Authority: Singapore Statutes Online / PDPC
Source URL: https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/PDPA2012-S64-2021
Topics: security_breach, notice_transparency, accountability
Keywords: data breach notification, notifiable data breach, PDPC Singapore

## Summary
Singapore breach-notification regulations specifying notification and assessment requirements under PDPA.

## Key compliance obligations
- Assess whether a data breach is notifiable.
- Notify PDPC and affected individuals where required.
- Maintain evidence of breach assessment and response.
