## Summary
Singapore's personal data framework with consent, purpose limitation, notification, access and correction, accuracy, protection, retention limitation, transfer limitation, accountability, breach notification, and DNC obligations.

## Key compliance obligations
- Meet consent, purpose limitation, and notification obligations.
- Protect personal data and cease retention when no longer needed.
- Apply transfer limitation controls for overseas transfers.
- Notify qualifying data breaches and respect Do Not Call rules.
