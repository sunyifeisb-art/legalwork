Jurisdiction: Mauritius
Region: MU

Keywords: Mauritius Data Protection Act 2017, Data Protection Office, DPO

## Summary
Mauritius data protection law aligned with GDPR-style principles, including lawful basis, rights, DPO, transfer, and breach obligations.

## Key compliance obligations
- Identify lawful basis and controller/processor duties.
- Support rights and breach notification.
- Assess transfers and registration where applicable.
