Jurisdiction: Denmark

## Summary
Danish act supplementing GDPR with national rules and derogations.

## Key compliance obligations
- Check Danish national processing rules and exceptions.
- Use Datatilsynet guidance for local implementation.
- Assess employee and national identification data carefully.
