# Iowa Consumer Data Protection Act

Jurisdiction: United States - Iowa
Region: US-IA
Citation: Iowa Consumer Data Protection Act
Authority: Iowa Legislature
Source URL: https://www.legis.iowa.gov/legislation/BillBook?ba=SF262&ga=90
Topics: notice_transparency, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, third_party_sharing, marketing_opt_out
Keywords: Iowa Consumer Data Protection Act, ICDPA, consumer rights

## Summary
Iowa consumer privacy law with privacy notices, consumer access/deletion/portability rights, and sale opt-out duties.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide clear notices and rights mechanisms.
- Disclose categories of data and third-party disclosures.
- Offer sale opt-out where applicable.
