# Organic Law on the Protection of Personal Data

Jurisdiction: Tunisia
Region: TN
Citation: Organic Law No. 2004-63 on the Protection of Personal Data
Authority: INPDP Tunisia
Source URL: http://www.inpdp.nat.tn/
Topics: notice_transparency, consent, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, cross_border_transfer, accountability
Keywords: Tunisia personal data protection, INPDP, Organic Law 2004-63

## Summary
Tunisia's personal data protection law with authority oversight, consent, authorisation, rights, and transfer controls.

## Key compliance obligations
- Check authorisation and declaration requirements.
- Provide notices and rights handling.
- Assess sensitive data and cross-border transfers.
