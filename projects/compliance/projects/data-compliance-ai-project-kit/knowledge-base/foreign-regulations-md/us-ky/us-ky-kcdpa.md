## Summary
Kentucky comprehensive privacy law effective January 1, 2026, giving consumers rights and setting controller and processor duties.

## Key compliance obligations
- Support access, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out rights.
- Provide privacy notice and appeal mechanisms.
- Use processor contracts and assessments for high-risk processing.
