## Summary
Connecticut comprehensive privacy law with rights, controller duties, opt-outs, sensitive data consent, child data protections, and data protection assessments.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide privacy notice and rights mechanisms.
- Obtain consent for sensitive data and comply with child-data restrictions.
- Implement opt-out mechanisms for targeted advertising, sale, and profiling.
