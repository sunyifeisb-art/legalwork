## Summary
Montana comprehensive privacy law covering consumer rights, opt-outs, sensitive data consent, universal opt-out recognition, and assessments.

## Key compliance obligations
- Support rights and opt-outs.
- Obtain consent for sensitive data.
- Conduct assessments for high-risk processing.
