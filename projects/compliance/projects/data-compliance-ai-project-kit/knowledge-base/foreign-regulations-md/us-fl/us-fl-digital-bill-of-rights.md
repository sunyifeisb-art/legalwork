# Florida Digital Bill of Rights

Jurisdiction: United States - Florida
Region: US-FL
Citation: Florida Digital Bill of Rights

Topics: notice_transparency, consent, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, marketing_opt_out, children_data, automated_decision_making, accountability
Keywords: Florida Digital Bill of Rights, FDBR, targeted advertising, sensitive data, consumer rights

## Summary
Florida's state privacy framework aimed at large digital businesses, with consumer rights, targeted advertising and sale opt-outs, sensitive-data controls, and child-data provisions.

## Key compliance obligations
- Support consumer privacy rights and appeals where applicable.
- Disclose processing purposes and sale or targeted-advertising practices.
- Apply sensitive-data and child-data controls under the statute.
