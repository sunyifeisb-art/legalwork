## Summary
Mexico's private-sector personal data law covering privacy notices, consent, ARCO rights, sensitive data, transfers, security, and INAI enforcement.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide privacy notices and identify data processing purposes.
- Obtain consent where required, especially for sensitive data.
- Support access, rectification, cancellation, and opposition rights.
- Control transfers and maintain security measures.
