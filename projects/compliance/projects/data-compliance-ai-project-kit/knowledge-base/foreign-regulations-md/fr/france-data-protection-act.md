# French Data Protection Act

Jurisdiction: France
Region: FR
Citation: Loi n° 78-17 du 6 janvier 1978 relative à l'informatique, aux fichiers et aux libertés
Authority: CNIL / Légifrance
Source URL: https://www.cnil.fr/en/official-texts
Topics: lawful_basis, sensitive_data, children_data, data_subject_rights, security_breach, accountability
Keywords: French Data Protection Act, CNIL, Loi Informatique et Libertés

## Summary
France's national data protection act, adapted to GDPR, with national provisions on CNIL powers, health data, children, sensitive processing, and enforcement.

## Key compliance obligations
- Apply French national supplements alongside GDPR.
- Check CNIL rules and authorisations for sensitive or health processing.
- Track French-specific age and rights requirements.
