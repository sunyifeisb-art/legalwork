## Summary
UAE federal personal data law for controllers and processors, consent and processing controls, data subject rights, DPO-related duties, breach notice, and cross-border transfers.

## Key compliance obligations
- Process personal data under lawful conditions and transparent notices.
- Support rights to access, correction, restriction, erasure, and portability.
- Use processor controls and security measures.
- Assess cross-border transfer conditions.
