## Summary
Indonesia's personal data protection law covering personal data controller and processor obligations, lawful processing bases, data subject rights, breach notification, cross-border transfers, and sanctions.

## Key compliance obligations
- Process personal data on a lawful basis and for specified purposes.
- Support data subject rights and correction/deletion mechanisms.
- Notify breaches and apply security safeguards.
- Meet cross-border transfer requirements.
