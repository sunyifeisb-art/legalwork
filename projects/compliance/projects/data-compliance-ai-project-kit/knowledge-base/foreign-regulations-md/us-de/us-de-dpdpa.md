## Summary
Delaware consumer privacy law covering consumer rights, notices, sensitive data, opt-outs, and data protection assessments.

## Key compliance obligations
- Publish privacy notices and support rights.
- Control sensitive and child-related processing.
- Perform assessments for high-risk processing.
