## Summary
New Hampshire comprehensive privacy law with consumer rights, opt-outs, controller duties, and sensitive data requirements.

## Key compliance obligations
- Offer rights and appeals.
- Provide clear privacy notices.
- Perform assessments for covered high-risk processing.
