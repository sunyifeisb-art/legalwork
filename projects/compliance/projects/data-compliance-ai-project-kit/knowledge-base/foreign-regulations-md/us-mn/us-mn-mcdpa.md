## Summary
Minnesota privacy law effective July 31, 2025, providing consumer rights, transparency, opt-outs, sensitive-data requirements, and profiling-related controls.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide privacy notices and consumer rights mechanisms.
- Support opt-outs for sale, targeted advertising, and certain profiling.
- Conduct and retain data protection assessments where required.
