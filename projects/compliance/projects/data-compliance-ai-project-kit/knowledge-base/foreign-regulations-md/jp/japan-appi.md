## Summary
Japan's personal information law governing purpose specification, restrictions on use, sensitive information, third-party provision, foreign transfers, data subject requests, pseudonymised and anonymously processed information, and PPC supervision.

## Key compliance obligations
- Specify and disclose purposes of use.
- Control third-party provision and foreign transfers.
- Handle special care-required personal information with appropriate basis.
- Respond to disclosure, correction, and suspension requests.
