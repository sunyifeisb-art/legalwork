# Personal Data Protection Law

Jurisdiction: Egypt
Region: EG
Citation: Law No. 151 of 2020 Promulgating the Personal Data Protection Law
Authority: Egyptian Government / MCIT
Source URL: https://mcit.gov.eg/en/Media_Center/Press_Room/Press_Releases/53388
Topics: consent, notice_transparency, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, cross_border_transfer, security_breach
Keywords: Egypt Personal Data Protection Law, Law 151 of 2020, data protection centre

## Summary
Egypt's personal data protection law regulating electronic personal data processing, controller/processor duties, rights, transfers, and licensing concepts.

## Key compliance obligations
- Identify consent or legal basis and provide notices.
- Assess sensitive data and cross-border transfer controls.
- Track executive regulations and licensing requirements.
