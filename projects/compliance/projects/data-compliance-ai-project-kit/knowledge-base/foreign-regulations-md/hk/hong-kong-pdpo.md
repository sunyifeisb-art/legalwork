# Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance

Jurisdiction: Hong Kong SAR
Region: HK
Citation: Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance
Authority: Hong Kong e-Legislation / PCPD
Source URL: https://www.elegislation.gov.hk/hk/cap486
Topics: notice_transparency, data_subject_rights, third_party_sharing, retention_deletion, security_breach, marketing_opt_out
Keywords: PDPO, Hong Kong privacy, data protection principles, PCPD

## Summary
Hong Kong personal data privacy ordinance based on data protection principles, rights, direct marketing rules, and PCPD enforcement.

## Key compliance obligations
- Follow data protection principles for collection, use, retention, security, openness, and access/correction.
- Provide personal information collection statements.
- Comply with direct marketing consent and opt-out requirements.
