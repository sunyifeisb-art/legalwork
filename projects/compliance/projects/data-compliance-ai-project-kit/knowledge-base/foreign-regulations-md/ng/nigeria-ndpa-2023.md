Jurisdiction: Nigeria

## Summary
Nigeria's national data protection law with lawful bases, controller and processor obligations, data subject rights, transfer safeguards, security and breach duties, and NDPC oversight.

## Key compliance obligations
- Process personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
- Limit processing and retention to necessary purposes.
- Support data subject rights and complaints.
- Use transfer safeguards and appropriate security.
