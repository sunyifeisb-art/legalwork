## Summary
Germany's federal data protection law supplementing the GDPR, with rules on public-sector processing, employee data, special categories, scoring, video surveillance, and supervisory authorities.

## Key compliance obligations
- Check BDSG rules in addition to GDPR for German processing.
- Assess employee-data and special-category processing under German supplements.
- Track German DPO and supervisory-authority requirements.
