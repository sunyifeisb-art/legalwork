## Summary
Colorado comprehensive consumer privacy law with controller duties, processor contracts, rights, sensitive-data consent, opt-out for sale, targeted advertising, and profiling, plus data protection assessments.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide clear privacy notice and controller contact information.
- Support consumer rights to access, correct, delete, obtain data, and opt out.
- Obtain consent for sensitive data and perform data protection assessments for high-risk processing.
- Recognise required universal opt-out mechanisms.
