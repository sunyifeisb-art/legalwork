## Summary
Brazil's general personal data protection law with legal bases, principles, data subject rights, international transfer rules, processing-agent duties, breach duties, and ANPD enforcement.

## Key compliance obligations
- Document legal bases and purposes for processing.
- Protect sensitive and children's data with heightened care.
- Enable data subject rights and transparent notices.
- Use contractual and legal safeguards for operators and international transfers.
