## Summary
ICO statutory code for online services likely to be accessed by children, focused on privacy-by-design and child safeguards.

## Key compliance obligations
- Put children's best interests at the centre of design.
- Use high privacy settings by default.
- Limit profiling, geolocation, and nudge techniques for children.
