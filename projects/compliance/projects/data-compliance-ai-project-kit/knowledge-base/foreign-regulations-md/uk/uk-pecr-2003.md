# Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations

Jurisdiction: United Kingdom
Region: UK

Authority: legislation.gov.uk / ICO

Keywords: PECR, cookies, electronic marketing, traffic data, location data

## Summary
UK e-privacy regime for cookies, electronic marketing, communications confidentiality, and related telecom privacy duties.

## Key compliance obligations
- Obtain cookie consent where required.
- Respect direct-marketing consent and opt-out rules.
- Protect communications traffic and location data.
