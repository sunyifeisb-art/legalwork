## Summary
South Korea's comprehensive personal information law, covering consent, processing limits, sensitive and unique identifying information, rights, cross-border transfers, security measures, breach notification, and PIPC oversight.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide notices and obtain consent where required for collection, use, provision, and overseas transfers.
- Apply heightened controls to sensitive and unique identifying information.
- Support rights to access, correction, deletion, and suspension.
- Maintain technical, managerial, and physical safeguards.
