## Summary
Swiss revised data protection law with transparency duties, privacy by design/default, processing records, data security, breach notification, foreign transfer controls, and automated individual decision information duties.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide privacy information and protect data by design and by default.
- Maintain records of processing where applicable.
- Assess high-risk processing and notify serious data security breaches.
- Use adequate safeguards for foreign transfers.
