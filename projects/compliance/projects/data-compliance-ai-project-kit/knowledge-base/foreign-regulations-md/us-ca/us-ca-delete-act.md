# California Delete Act

Jurisdiction: United States - California
Region: US-CA

Authority: California Legislature / CPPA

Keywords: California Delete Act, data broker registry, deletion mechanism, CPPA

## Summary
California data-broker law creating additional registration and deletion obligations, including a centralized deletion mechanism.

## Key compliance obligations
- Assess data-broker registration obligations.
- Prepare for centralized deletion request handling.
- Maintain auditable deletion and opt-out workflows.
