# EDPB Guidelines on Consent under GDPR

Jurisdiction: European Union / EEA
Region: EU
Citation: Guidelines 05/2020 on consent under Regulation 2016/679
Authority: European Data Protection Board
Source URL: https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/guidelines/guidelines-052020-consent-under-regulation-2016679_en
Topics: consent, notice_transparency, data_subject_rights, marketing_opt_out
Keywords: EDPB consent guidelines, freely given, specific informed unambiguous, withdraw consent

## Summary
EDPB guidance explaining valid GDPR consent, including freely given, specific, informed, unambiguous, explicit consent, and withdrawal.

## Key compliance obligations
- Avoid bundled or coerced consent.
- Make withdrawal as easy as giving consent.
- Keep consent records and separate consent from contract acceptance.
