# NIS2 Directive

Jurisdiction: European Union / EEA
Region: EU
Citation: Directive (EU) 2022/2555
Authority: European Union
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32022L2555
Topics: security_breach, accountability, processor_contracts
Keywords: NIS2, cybersecurity risk management, incident reporting, essential entities, important entities

## Summary
EU cybersecurity directive for essential and important entities, relevant to data security governance and incident response.

## Key compliance obligations
- Maintain cybersecurity risk-management measures.
- Report significant incidents under NIS2 timelines.
- Manage supply-chain and vendor cybersecurity risks.
