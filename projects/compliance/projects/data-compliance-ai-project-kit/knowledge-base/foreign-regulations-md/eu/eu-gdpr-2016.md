## Summary
Core EU data protection regime for processing personal data. Establishes principles, legal bases, data subject rights, controller and processor obligations, breach notice, DPIA, and international transfer controls.

## Key compliance obligations
- Identify a lawful basis before processing personal data and disclose purposes transparently.
- Limit processing to specified, explicit, legitimate purposes and necessary data.
- Apply additional safeguards for special-category and children data.
- Use processor contracts and maintain accountability records.
- Use adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, binding corporate rules, or other safeguards for international transfers.
- Support access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and automated decision rights.
