# EU-US Data Privacy Framework Adequacy Decision

Jurisdiction: European Union / EEA
Region: EU
Citation: Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2023/1795
Authority: European Commission
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32023D1795
Topics: cross_border_transfer, third_party_sharing, accountability
Keywords: EU-US Data Privacy Framework, adequacy decision, DPF, United States transfer

## Summary
EU adequacy decision enabling certain transfers to certified US organisations under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

## Key compliance obligations
- Verify recipient certification before relying on DPF.
- Document transfer mechanism and scope.
- Monitor onward-transfer and certification status.
