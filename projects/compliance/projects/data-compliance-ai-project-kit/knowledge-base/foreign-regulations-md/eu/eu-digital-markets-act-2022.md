# Digital Markets Act

Jurisdiction: European Union / EEA
Region: EU
Citation: Regulation (EU) 2022/1925
Authority: European Union
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32022R1925
Topics: consent, third_party_sharing, data_subject_rights, data_minimization, accountability
Keywords: DMA, gatekeeper, combining personal data, data portability, business users

## Summary
EU gatekeeper regulation with restrictions on combining personal data and duties around access, portability, and fairness.

## Key compliance obligations
- Check gatekeeper restrictions before combining data across services.
- Support portability and access duties for covered platform data.
- Maintain auditable compliance with gatekeeper obligations.
