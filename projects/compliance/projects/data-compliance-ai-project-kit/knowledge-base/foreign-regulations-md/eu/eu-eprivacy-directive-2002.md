## Summary
EU privacy framework for electronic communications, cookies, terminal equipment access, traffic and location data, and direct marketing communications.

## Key compliance obligations
- Obtain required consent for storing or accessing information on user devices except for narrow exemptions.
- Respect confidentiality of electronic communications and location or traffic data limits.
- Provide opt-out and consent controls for direct marketing communications.
