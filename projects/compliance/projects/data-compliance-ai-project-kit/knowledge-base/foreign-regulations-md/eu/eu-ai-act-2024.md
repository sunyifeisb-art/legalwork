## Summary
EU horizontal AI regulation with prohibitions, high-risk AI obligations, transparency duties, governance requirements, and links to data protection compliance for AI systems.

## Key compliance obligations
- Classify AI use cases and identify prohibited, high-risk, limited-risk, or GPAI obligations.
- For high-risk AI, maintain risk management, data governance, technical documentation, logging, transparency, human oversight, accuracy, robustness, and cybersecurity measures.
- Disclose required information for certain AI interactions, synthetic content, and biometric categorisation or emotion-recognition contexts.
