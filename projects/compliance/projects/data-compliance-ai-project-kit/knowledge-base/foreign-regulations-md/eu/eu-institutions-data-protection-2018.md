# Data Protection for EU Institutions, Bodies, Offices and Agencies

Jurisdiction: European Union / EEA
Region: EU
Citation: Regulation (EU) 2018/1725
Authority: European Union
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32018R1725
Topics: lawful_basis, notice_transparency, data_subject_rights, processor_contracts, cross_border_transfer, accountability
Keywords: EU institutions, EDPS, Regulation 2018/1725, personal data

## Summary
Data protection regime for EU institutions and bodies, aligned with GDPR concepts and supervised by the EDPS.

## Key compliance obligations
- Apply GDPR-like principles to EU institutional processing.
- Maintain accountability, DPO, and rights-handling mechanisms.
- Use safeguards for transfers and processors.
