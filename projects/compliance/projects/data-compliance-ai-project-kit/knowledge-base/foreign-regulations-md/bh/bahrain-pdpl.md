# Personal Data Protection Law

Jurisdiction: Bahrain
Region: BH
Citation: Law No. 30 of 2018 Promulgating the Personal Data Protection Law
Authority: Bahrain Personal Data Protection Authority
Source URL: https://www.pdp.gov.bh/en/Legislation
Topics: lawful_basis, notice_transparency, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, cross_border_transfer, security_breach
Keywords: Bahrain PDPL, Personal Data Protection Authority, Law 30 of 2018

## Summary
Bahrain data protection law with controller duties, rights, transfer rules, sensitive processing, and authority oversight.

## Key compliance obligations
- Identify processing basis and provide notices.
- Support rights and objections.
- Assess cross-border transfers and sensitive-data authorisations.
