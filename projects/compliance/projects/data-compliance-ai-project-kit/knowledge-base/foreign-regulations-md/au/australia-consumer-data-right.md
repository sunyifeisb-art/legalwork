# Consumer Data Right Framework

Jurisdiction: Australia
Region: AU

## Summary
Australian data-sharing framework giving consumers rights to direct transfer of specified data to accredited recipients.

## Key compliance obligations
- Identify whether CDR sector rules apply.
- Manage consent, authorisation, and disclosure flows.
- Meet accreditation and information-security duties.
