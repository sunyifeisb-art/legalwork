# Spam Act 2003

Jurisdiction: Australia
Region: AU
Citation: Spam Act 2003
Authority: Federal Register of Legislation / ACMA
Source URL: https://www.legislation.gov.au/C2004A01214/latest/text
Topics: consent, marketing_opt_out, notice_transparency
Keywords: Spam Act, commercial electronic messages, unsubscribe, consent

## Summary
Australian electronic marketing law requiring consent, sender identification, and unsubscribe facilities for commercial electronic messages.

## Key compliance obligations
- Send commercial electronic messages only with required consent.
- Identify the sender accurately.
- Provide functional unsubscribe facilities.
