## Summary
Australian privacy framework built around the Australian Privacy Principles, covering collection notice, use/disclosure, direct marketing, cross-border disclosure, access/correction, security, and notifiable data breaches.

## Key compliance obligations
- Comply with Australian Privacy Principles.
- Provide collection notices and limit use and disclosure.
- Apply APP 8 controls before cross-border disclosure.
- Notify eligible data breaches under the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.
