## Summary
India's digital personal data law covering consent, legitimate uses, notices, data fiduciary duties, data principal rights, children's data, cross-border transfer restrictions, and Data Protection Board enforcement.

## Key compliance obligations
- Provide notice and obtain valid consent unless a legitimate use applies.
- Limit retention after purpose completion or consent withdrawal.
- Protect children's data and avoid tracking or behavioural monitoring of children where prohibited.
- Implement security safeguards and report personal data breaches.
