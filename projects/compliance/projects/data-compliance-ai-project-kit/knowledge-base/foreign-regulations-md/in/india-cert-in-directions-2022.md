## Summary
Indian cybersecurity directions relevant to incident reporting and log retention for covered entities.

## Key compliance obligations
- Report specified cyber incidents to CERT-In within required timelines.
- Synchronise clocks and retain logs as required.
- Maintain incident-response evidence.
