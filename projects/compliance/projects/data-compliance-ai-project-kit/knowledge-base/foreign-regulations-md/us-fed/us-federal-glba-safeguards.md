# GLBA Safeguards Rule

Jurisdiction: United States - Federal
Region: US-FED
Citation: Standards for Safeguarding Customer Information
Authority: Federal Trade Commission / eCFR
Source URL: https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-16/chapter-I/subchapter-C/part-314
Topics: security_breach, processor_contracts, accountability, sensitive_data
Keywords: GLBA, Safeguards Rule, customer information, financial institutions

## Summary
US financial privacy security rule requiring covered financial institutions to maintain an information security program.

## Key compliance obligations
- Design and maintain a written information security program.
- Assess risks and implement safeguards.
- Oversee service providers and monitor controls.
