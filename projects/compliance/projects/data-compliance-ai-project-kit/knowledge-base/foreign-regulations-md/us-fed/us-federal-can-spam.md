# CAN-SPAM Act and Rule

Jurisdiction: United States - Federal
Region: US-FED
Citation: CAN-SPAM Rule
Authority: FTC / eCFR
Source URL: https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-16/chapter-I/subchapter-C/part-316
Topics: marketing_opt_out, notice_transparency, consent
Keywords: CAN-SPAM, commercial email, unsubscribe, header information

## Summary
US commercial email law and FTC rule covering accurate sender information, advertising identification, and opt-out handling.

## Key compliance obligations
- Avoid deceptive headers and subject lines.
- Include required sender identification and opt-out mechanism.
- Honor opt-out requests within required timeframes.
