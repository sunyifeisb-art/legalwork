# Fair Credit Reporting Act

Jurisdiction: United States - Federal
Region: US-FED
Citation: Fair Credit Reporting Act
Authority: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau / FTC
Source URL: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/rules-policy/regulations/1022/
Topics: data_subject_rights, notice_transparency, third_party_sharing, data_minimization
Keywords: FCRA, consumer report, credit reporting, adverse action, permissible purpose

## Summary
US credit-reporting privacy law restricting consumer-report use and providing dispute, disclosure, and adverse-action rights.

## Key compliance obligations
- Use consumer reports only for permissible purposes.
- Provide required notices and adverse-action information.
- Support dispute and correction processes.
