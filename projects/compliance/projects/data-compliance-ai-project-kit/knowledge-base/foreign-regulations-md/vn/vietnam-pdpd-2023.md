## Summary
Vietnam personal data protection decree with consent, rights, sensitive data, impact assessment, cross-border transfer, and breach obligations.

## Key compliance obligations
- Obtain valid consent and provide processing notices.
- Prepare personal data processing impact assessments where required.
- Meet cross-border transfer documentation and notification expectations.
