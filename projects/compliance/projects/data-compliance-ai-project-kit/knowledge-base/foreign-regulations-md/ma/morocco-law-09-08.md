# Law No. 09-08 on Personal Data Protection

Jurisdiction: Morocco
Region: MA
Citation: Law No. 09-08 relating to the protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data
Authority: CNDP Morocco
Source URL: https://www.cndp.ma/en/legal-framework/law.html
Topics: notice_transparency, consent, sensitive_data, data_subject_rights, cross_border_transfer, accountability
Keywords: Morocco Law 09-08, CNDP, personal data protection

## Summary
Morocco's personal data protection law with CNDP oversight, declarations/authorisations, rights, sensitive data, and transfer controls.

## Key compliance obligations
- Assess declaration or authorisation obligations.
- Provide notice and rights procedures.
- Control sensitive data and transfers abroad.
